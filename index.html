STORIES BEHIND THE ART

The Inspiration & Process

GOLDEN FEATHERS

This intricate paper art piece features hand-cut feathers in cream and gold tones layered on black paper. Each feather required careful cutting and shaping to create the delicate, flowing movement. The contrast between the golden warmth and black background symbolizes finding light in dark moments. The X-formation creates balance and symmetry, representing the intersection of different paths in life.

WIRE FLOWERS

These sculptural wire flowers are crafted from brass and copper wire, each one hand-shaped with patience and precision. The flowers vary in size and complexity, exploring how simple materials can create organic forms. What started as an experiment in line and space became a meditation on growth and blooming. Each petal required careful bending to maintain both structure and grace.

TROPICAL PARROT

This colored pencil piece took over 15 hours to complete, with each feather requiring multiple layers of blue, green, and turquoise to achieve the iridescent quality. The challenge was capturing the parrot's personality - that intelligent, curious gaze. Working on this taught me patience; colored pencil doesn't allow for rushing. Every stroke had to be intentional, building up color gradually to create depth and dimension.

LOYAL COMPANION

This circular paper cutout captures the pure joy of a happy dog. The piece required extreme precision - one wrong cut would ruin hours of work. I chose the circular format to symbolize the complete, unconditional nature of a dog's love. The challenge was maintaining the structural integrity while creating flowing, organic lines for the fur. Created with an exacto knife and steady hands, this became a tribute to the unwavering loyalty of our four-legged friends.

Each artwork tells a story. Follow @snippetandsplotch for more behind-the-scenes content.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

HOW DO I PURCHASE AN ARTWORK?

Simply contact me via email at snippetandsploch@gmail.com or send a direct message on Instagram @snippetandsplotch with the artwork title you're interested in. I'll respond within 24 hours with availability, payment options, and shipping details.

DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?

Yes! I ship worldwide. All artworks are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive safely. Shipping costs vary by location and will be calculated based on your address. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days.

ARE THE ARTWORKS FRAMED?

Most artworks are sold unframed to allow you to choose framing that matches your space. However, I can recommend local framers or provide framing services for an additional fee. Each piece comes with care instructions and framing suggestions.

DO YOU ACCEPT COMMISSIONS?

Yes! I love creating custom pieces. Commission work typically requires a 50% deposit upfront, with the remainder due upon completion. The process includes initial consultations, sketches for approval, and regular updates. Timeline varies by project complexity, usually 2-6 weeks.

WHAT'S YOUR RETURN POLICY?

I want you to love your artwork! If you're not completely satisfied, you may return it within 14 days of delivery for a full refund (minus shipping costs). The artwork must be in its original condition. Custom commissioned pieces are non-refundable.

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?

I accept PayPal, Venmo, bank transfers, and major credit cards. For international orders, PayPal is preferred. Payment plans can be arranged for purchases over $500 - please inquire for details.

IS EACH PIECE AN ORIGINAL OR A PRINT?

Each artwork listing clearly states whether it's an original or a print. Original pieces are one-of-a-kind and come with a certificate of authenticity. Prints are high-quality reproductions, often in limited editions. All originals are signed and dated on the back.

HOW DO I CARE FOR MY ARTWORK?

Each artwork comes with specific care instructions based on its medium. Generally, keep artwork away from direct sunlight, excessive humidity, and heat. For paper-based art, use UV-protective glass when framing. Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth. Never use cleaning products directly on the artwork.

CAN I SEE THE ARTWORK IN PERSON BEFORE BUYING?

While I don't have a permanent gallery space, I occasionally participate in local art shows and open studio events. Follow me on Instagram @snippetandsplotch for announcements. I'm also happy to provide additional photos or video of specific pieces upon request.

DO YOU OFFER GIFT CERTIFICATES?

Yes! Gift certificates are available in any amount and make perfect gifts for art lovers. They can be used toward any artwork or custom commission. Contact me directly to arrange a gift certificate purchase.

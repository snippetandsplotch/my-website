About the Artist

Welcome to Snippet & Splotch! I'm a passionate artist dedicated to creating unique pieces that evoke emotion and tell stories. My work explores the intersection of color, texture, and form across various mediums.

Artistic Journey

With over 10 years of experience, I've developed a distinctive style that blends traditional techniques with contemporary themes.

Inspiration

Nature, urban landscapes, and human emotions serve as my primary sources of inspiration for creating meaningful artwork.
